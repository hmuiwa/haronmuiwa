Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary based on scope and complexity. A simple logo design takes 7-10 days, while a complete brand identity can take 2-4 weeks. I'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

What do I need to get started?

Just your vision and goals! I'll guide you through a discovery process to understand your business, target audience, and design preferences. Any existing brand materials or inspiration you have are helpful but not required.

Do you offer revisions?

Yes! All packages include revisions. The number varies by package, but I'm committed to ensuring you're completely satisfied with the final result.

What file formats will I receive?

You'll receive all necessary formats for both print and digital use, including AI, EPS, PDF, PNG, JPG, and SVG files. I ensure you have everything needed for any application.

Can you work with international clients?

Absolutely! While I'm based in Kenya, I work with clients worldwide. We can communicate via email, video calls, or your preferred method. Payment can be made via bank transfer, PayPal, or M-Pesa.

What is your payment structure?

I typically require 50% upfront to begin work and 50% upon completion. For larger projects, we can arrange milestone-based payments. All payment terms are discussed and agreed upon before starting.
